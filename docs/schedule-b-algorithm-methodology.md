## 1. QualityScore (range: 0.6 – 1.4)

**QualityScore** measures how well your videos keep people watching and interacting. Higher score = bigger share of the revenue pool.

**Exact formula:**
QualityScore = (Completion Rate × 0.30) + (Effective Engagement Rate × 0.25) + (View Distribution Entropy × 0.25) + (Retention Curve Fit × 0.20)

| Dimension | Weight | What it measures | High Score Example | Low Score Example |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Completion Rate** | 30% | % of viewers who watch to the end | 85%+ completion on a 60-second short | Only 25% finish the video |
| **Effective Engagement Rate** | 25% | Meaningful comments (≥15 characters, no spam) | 180 unique thoughtful comments | 12 one-word comments or emoji spam |
| **View Distribution Entropy** | 25% | How spread out your views are (time, countries, devices) | Views from 40 countries, all 24 hours | 95% of views from one country in 2 hours |
| **Retention Curve Fit** | 20% | How evenly people stay watching | Smooth watch curve – viewers stay till end | Big drop-off at 12 seconds |

**Pro tip:** You can check your exact numbers for every video in the Dashboard.

---

## 2. TrustScore (range: 0.6 – 1.3)

**TrustScore** looks at how consistent and reliable your content has been over the past 30 and 90 days.

- Rewards steady performance (no sudden spikes from bots or one viral hit that dies fast)
- Calculated using rolling windows: 30-day recent activity + 90-day longer-term pattern
- You'll see your current TrustScore and charts in the Dashboard

Higher TrustScore = more stable earnings month after month.

---

## 3. IP Decay Multiplier

This multiplier encourages quality over flooding the platform with the same characters/world.

**Rules (applied per IP – same main characters + story universe):**
- Videos 1–5: **1.0x** (full multiplier)
- Videos 6–15: **linear decay** down to **0.7x**
- Video 16 and beyond: **fixed 0.6x**

**Special reset rule:**
If an IP has been completely inactive for more than **180 days**, the multiplier **resets to 0.8x** for the next 5 videos when you return.

---

## 4. How to Improve Your Scores

1. **Boost Completion Rate** – Hook viewers in the first 3 seconds. Keep pacing tight. End strong.
2. **Increase Effective Engagement** – Ask real questions. Reply to comments. Avoid repetitive topics.
3. **Improve View Distribution** – Post at different times. Share on multiple platforms. Create stories for different audiences.
4. **Smooth out Retention Curve** – Front-load the best moment. Use cliffhangers. Test shorter vs. longer versions.
5. **Keep TrustScore high** – Post consistently. Never game metrics (bots = instant penalty).
